Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent Black River Landing for a private event?
To inquire about renting Black River Landing or other LPFA facilities for a private event, please use the contact form above and select "Facility Rental Inquiry" as your topic. Our team will follow up to discuss availability, rates, and requirements.
How do I book a boat tour or private charter?
Visit our Recreation page to learn about available tours and submit an inquiry. You can also call us at 440.204.2269 or email info@lorainport.com to check availability and pricing.
How do I learn about development financing opportunities?
Visit our Development & Finance page for an overview of our programs, including brownfields grants, property financing, and active RFPs. Contact our office directly to discuss a specific project or opportunity.
Is the Mile-Long Pier open to the public?
Yes, the Mile-Long Pier is generally open to the public for walking, fishing, and sightseeing, weather permitting. There is no admission fee. Please contact us for current accessibility and seasonal hours information.
How do I stay informed about upcoming events?
Subscribe to our newsletter using the form on our homepage, and follow us on Facebook and Instagram for the latest event announcements and waterfront news.
Is the Black River Wharf Boat Launch free to use?
Please contact our office at 440.204.2269 or info@lorainport.com for current information on boat launch fees, hours, and seasonal operation.
